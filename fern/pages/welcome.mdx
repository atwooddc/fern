## Motivation

<div className="motivation">
  Stripe, Twilio, and AWS have the resources to invest in internal tooling for developer 
  experience. They provide client libraries in multiple languages and up-to-date developer documentation. 

  We built Fern to productize this process and make it accessible. Just input an OpenAPI (f.k.a. Swagger) 
  Spec, and output idiomatic SDKs and beautiful documentation.
</div>
